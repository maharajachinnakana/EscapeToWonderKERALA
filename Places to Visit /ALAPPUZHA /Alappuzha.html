ABOUT

Alappuzha, also known as Alleppey, is famous for its serene backwaters, beautiful houseboats, and golden beaches. It’s one of Kerala’s top tourist destinations, offering houseboat cruises, shikara rides, and traditional village experiences that showcase the charm of “Venice of the East.

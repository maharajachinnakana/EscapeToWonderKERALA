Yellapetty is a small village located 35 kilometers from Munnar, on the border of Kerala and Tamil Nadu. It is known for its lush green hills, cascading waterfalls, and scenic beauty. The name Yellapetty means "last village" in Tamil.

Night Sky and Sunris

The night sky in Yellapetty is especially beautiful in the winter when it is full of stars. The village is also a great place to watch the sunrise.

Strawberries

Yellapetty is a major producer of strawberries, and there are several strawberry farms in the area where you can pick your own strawberries.

Camping

There are several campsites in Yellapetty where you can stay in tents. Some of the popular campsites include Cloud Farm, Camp Footprint, and Route 49 Adventures. To reach these campsites, you will need to do a short trek.
